About

Mission & Vision

Environmentalists, agriculturists, architects, IT experts and engineers shared a common vision to build a sustainable community of the future. The importance of engaging the community with diverse views and backgrounds, broad-based policies that aim to secure the social well-being of communities as well as the protection of the environment. Encouraging many people are more ready than we might assume to accept the types of changes urgently needed to planting.

Today, developers and investors are recognizing the potential consequences and risks from climate change as they mobilize to address the negative impacts of developing land considering our ecosystem as a whole. Optimizing the use of renewable energy is crucial to the challenges of reducing the carbon impact on the way we develop our land. We partnered with the agricultural community to give us a master plan on how to build a community that is sustainable in terms of architecture.

Our culture will never be tainted by our dreams, but it will push us to uphold our heritage while introducing the future of the Filipino community living in a sustainable village of the Future.

EM & A Consultancy.

Agriculture should be the center of development in the Philippines, our dream may be tough but our actions can still make a difference. Reinventing architecture that focuses on biodiversity and the environment is the key factor why EM & A Architectural Consultancy came to existence. The group concept of connecting agriculture to architecture shall be the main trust firm. Building on social responsibility shows the importance of seeing one's group as moral, one reason people act on protecting the environment to help create a more moral and caring society.

Our vision is to create sustainable communities where agriculture and architecture coexist harmoniously, fostering a deep connection between people and the land. By integrating green spaces, urban farming, and eco-friendly building practices, we aim to reduce the carbon footprint and enhance the quality of life for all. Our projects are designed with the future in mind, ensuring that the needs of both present and future generations are met without compromising the health of our planet.

We believe that true development goes beyond economic growth; it encompasses the well-being of the environment and the people who depend on it.

Services

Project Management

We manage a project from identifying requirements, establishing clear and achievable objectives, balancing competing demands of quality, scope, cost, and time, adapting specifications, plans, and approach to meet expectations of all key stakeholders including the client and the end-user.

Feasibility Studies & Planning

Our team focuses on a specific subject that really focuses on feasibility study and aims to provide an independent assessment that examines all aspects of a proposed project, including technical, economic, financial, legal, and environmental considerations.

Interior Design

Our team interior design concept is the central theme that all the design elements are focused upon. It exists as an idea first and is brought to reality through careful planning. At its best, an interior design concept is a visual theme that evokes a specific mood by strategically using color, space, and style.

Complete Project Presentation

Our architecture presentation emanates from who we are that communicates our ideas based on our plans, elevations and measurements. It should tell a story evoking emotions from the audience that is inspired by our clients confidence on what we are capable of.

Rendering & Promotional Materials

Our innovation on architectural rendering became our main trust. It is our process of creating two-dimensional and three-dimensional images of a proposed architectural design. The goal is to illustrate a lifelike experience of how a space or building will look before it is built, accurately representing design intent.
